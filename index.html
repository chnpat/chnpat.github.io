About Me and My Research Interests

I'm currently working as a researcher in academia, and I'm interested in every aspect of software engineering. information security, personal data protection, and identity & access Management are my bread and butter. In addition, I'm active in the group of people who use formal methods, such as formal specification, formal verification, and static program analysis. However, I am enthusiastic about blockchain, Bitcoin, self-sovereign identity, and consensus mechanisms as well as other forms of decentralized technology. The areas of natural language processing and machine learning are also areas in which I am actively expanding my knowledge and skillset.

In addition to my passion for learning, I am also an avid photographer and traveler who values documenting the world's wonders. My Instagram account is where I'd love to show off some of my work.
